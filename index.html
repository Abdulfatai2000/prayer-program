Who We Are

A Gathering for Finishers

This is a student-led prayer initiative born out of a deep burden for every student who has made ambitious plans—5.0 CGPA, completing projects, achieving goals—only to see those plans scatter midway. We believe God is calling students to break that cycle and step into a lifestyle of finishing strong.

Theme Scripture: "Commit your works to the Lord, and your plans will be established." — Proverbs 16:3

The Journey

3 Nights of Encounter

Night 1 · Wednesday

SURRENDER
Laying down self-reliant plans before God. Repentance from making plans without seeking His will. Committing every goal to the Lord.
📖 Proverbs 16:3 · James 4:13–15

Night 2 · Thursday

STRENGTH
Breaking weariness, distractions, and the spirit of procrastination. Receiving renewed strength to press forward without giving up.
📖 Isaiah 40:31 · Galatians 6:9

Night 3 · Friday

VICTORY
Establishing God-ordained goals. Covenant to finish strong. Commissioning and sending forth as finishers.
📖 Philippians 3:12–14 · Joshua 1:9
